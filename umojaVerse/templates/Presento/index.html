Our White Paper

Blockchain Technology

Our blockchain development training teaches the skills needed to build decentralized applications and systems using blockchain technology. Suitable for both new programmers and experienced developers, the course covers a range of topics including blockchain fundamentals, smart contract development, and decentralized application design. Through hands-on exercises and real-world case studies, participants will gain a deep understanding of the technical and business aspects of blockchain development and the ability to build their own decentralized application

Metaverse Technology

Our metaverse development training teaches individuals how to design and build immersive virtual worlds using metaverse technology. The course covers 3D modeling and environment development, custom metaverse platform creation, and the integration of metaverse solutions into existing systems. Suitable for both new programmers and experienced developers, the training includes hands-on exercises, real-world case studies, and interactive lectures to provide a deep understanding of the technical and creative aspects of metaverse development and the ability to build their own metaverse applications.

Mentorship Programs

Our WB3 mentorship program provides personalized coaching and support for individuals looking to succeed in the world of Web3 technologies. One-on-one mentorship with industry experts, access to resources and networking opportunities, and a focus on practical skills and real-world applications help participants achieve their goals and succeed in the rapidly-evolving world of blockchain and metaverse development. Suitable for beginners and experienced professionals.

Lima Smart

Lima Smart is a product that uses smart contracts to solve problems in the Kenyan agricultural industry by streamlining and optimizing processes through technology, leading to increased efficiency and profitability. It automates and manages complex tasks, uses smart contracts for transparency and security, and is suitable for small farmers and large agricultural corporations.
